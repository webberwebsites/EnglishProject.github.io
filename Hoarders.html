Ode To My Toys

To my hundreds of toys-- I love you

I don’t know what I’d do without you Buzz Lightyear
Or you Thomas the Tank Engine
Or you Red Power Ranger
Or any of my toys

I have so many toys and I just want more!
I would never sell my beloved toys
Nothing in this world means more to me than my toys
I love my toys
